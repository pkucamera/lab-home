Principal Investigator

Boxin Shi / 施柏鑫

Assistant Professor (1000 Youth) /
特聘研究员(青年千人, 博士生导师)
shiboxin =AT= pku.edu.cn

Boxin Shi is currently a faculty member at Peking University. Before joining PKU in 2017, he has worked at the Artificial Intelligence Research Center, National Institute of Advanced Industrial Science and Technology (AIST) as a Researcher in 2016. From 2013 to 2016, he did postdoctoral research at MIT Media Lab, Singapore University of Technology and Design, and Nanyang Technological University.

Boxin earned his Ph.D. degree in information science and technology from the University of Tokyo in 2013 (supervised by Prof. Katsushi Ikeuchi), Master of Engineering from Peking University and Bachelor of Engineering from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, China, in 2010 and 2007 respectively.
